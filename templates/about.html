Who We Are

Flaming Sword Ministry International is a Christ-centered ministry committed to raising disciples, transforming lives through the Word of God, and equipping believers to fulfill God's purpose.

Our Vision

To raise Kingdom Ambassadors who demonstrate the love, power, and character of Jesus Christ throughout the world.

Our Mission

To preach the Gospel, disciple believers, empower leaders, and impact communities through worship, prayer, evangelism, and sound biblical teaching.
